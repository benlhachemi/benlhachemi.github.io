Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2025

Welcome to Dynamic Wallpaper. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data.

Information We Don't Collect

Our application does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. We do not:

  • Collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or location
  • Track your usage or behavior within the application
  • Access your files or content beyond what you explicitly select for use as wallpaper

Local Storage

Dynamic Wallpaper stores your preferences (such as selected wallpaper, theme settings, and accent color) locally on your device. This information never leaves your device and is used solely to provide the application's functionality.

System Access

Our application requires certain system permissions to function properly:

  • Access to change your desktop wallpaper
  • Ability to retrieve system accent color information
  • Ability to launch automatically on startup (if enabled)

These permissions are used exclusively for the intended functionality of the application and not for collecting or transmitting any data.

Third-Party Services

Dynamic Wallpaper does not integrate with or utilize any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
